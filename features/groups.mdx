title: "Groups"
description: "Create and manage private music-sharing circles"
---

A group is a private space where members can upload and download tracks. Only people you invite can see the group's tracks or join as members. Every track you upload is tied to a specific group, so your music stays within the audience you choose.

<Info>
  Groups are invite-only. Anyone with a Snipit account can be invited, but no one can discover or join a group without an invitation link from a current member.
</Info>

## Creating a group

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Create Group dialog">
    From your dashboard, click the **Create Group** button in the top-right area of the groups section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter a group name">
    Type a name in the **Group Name** field. This field is required — the form will not submit without it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a description (optional)">
    Use the **Description** field to give members context about the group's purpose, for example: *"Unreleased demos from the band"* or *"Local files for our shared Spotify playlist"*.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit the form">
    Click **Create Group**. The group is created immediately and appears on your dashboard. You are automatically added as the first member.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Viewing your groups

All groups you belong to — whether you created them or joined via an invite — appear on your dashboard as cards. Each card shows the group name and description.

Click a group card to open the group page, which shows:

- The group name and description in the header
- The total member count and track count
- A **Shared Tracks** list with all uploaded files
- A **Members** sidebar listing every member and their join date

## Group page actions

From the group page header you can:

- Click **Upload Track** to add a new audio file to the group
- Click **Invite Members** to generate an invite link and share it with others

## Group data model

Groups are stored in the `groups` table in Supabase with the following fields:

| Field | Type | Description |
|---|---|---|
| `id` | `string` | Unique identifier (UUID) for the group |
| `name` | `string` | Display name of the group (required) |
| `description` | `string \| null` | Optional description of the group's purpose |
| `created_by` | `string \| null` | Email address of the user who created the group |
| `created_at` | `string` | ISO 8601 timestamp of when the group was created |

Membership is tracked separately in the `group_members` table, which links a `member_id` (user UUID) to a `group_id` along with a `joined_at` timestamp.

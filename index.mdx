## What is Snipit?

Snipit is a music-sharing platform that lets you create private groups, upload your local music files, and share them with friends and family. It's perfect for sharing personal music collections, rare finds, and tracks that work as Spotify local files.

## Get started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an account">
    Sign up at [snipit.app](https://github.com/ehcaw/snippet) using your email address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create your first group">
    After logging in, go to your dashboard and click **Create Group** to set up a music-sharing circle.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload a track">
    Open your group and click **Upload Track** to share your first MP3 file with the group.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite your friends">
    Click **Invite Members** inside your group to send email invitations to friends.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Key features

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Private groups" icon="lock">
    All music sharing happens within private groups — only invited members can access shared tracks.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Spotify integration" icon="music">
    Download shared tracks and add them to Spotify as local files to integrate with your playlists.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Cross-group dashboard" icon="layout-dashboard">
    Your dashboard shows recently uploaded tracks from all your groups in one view.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Secure file storage" icon="shield">
    Files are stored securely in Supabase Storage and only accessible to group members.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Email invitations" icon="envelope">
    Invite members by email with a single click — no complex setup required.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Track metadata" icon="tag">
    Tracks are stored with title, artist, upload date, and uploader info for easy discovery.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

title: "Quickstart"
description: "Create your account, set up a group, and share your first track in under 5 minutes"
---

This guide walks you through your first end-to-end Snipit workflow: sign up, create a group, upload a track, and invite a friend.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an account">
    Go to the Snipit homepage and click **Get Started** or **Sign Up Now**.

    On the login page, enter your email address and password to create your account. Snipit uses Supabase Auth for secure email/password authentication.

    <Note>
      If you were invited to a group via an invite link, open that link first. After signing up you will be automatically added to the group.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create your first group">
    After logging in you land on your **Dashboard**. Click the **Create Group** button in the top-right of the "Your Groups" section.

    Fill in:
    - **Name** (required) — give your group a descriptive name, e.g. "Road Trip Playlist"
    - **Description** (optional) — a short note about what music you plan to share

    Click **Create** to confirm. Your new group appears in the dashboard grid.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open your group">
    Click on the group card from your dashboard to open the group page. Here you can see:
    - **Shared Tracks** — all tracks uploaded to this group
    - **Members** — everyone who has joined
    - Member count and track count in the header stats
  </Step>

  <Step title="Upload a track">
    Inside your group, click **Upload Track**. The upload dialog opens.

    Fill in all four required fields:
    - **Music File** — click **Select File** or drag-and-drop an MP3 or MP4 file (up to 50 MB)
    - **Track Title** — the song title, e.g. "Midnight Drive"
    - **Artist** — the artist name, e.g. "Various Artists"
    - **Share with Group** — select the group you just created from the dropdown

    Click **Upload Track**. The file uploads to secure storage and the track appears in the group's track list immediately.

    <Note>
      Accepted file types: `.mp3` (`audio/mpeg`), `.mp4` (`audio/mp4`, `video/mp4`). Files larger than 50 MB are not supported.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Invite a friend">
    Inside your group, click **Invite Members**. A dialog appears showing a unique invite link:

    ```
    https://your-snipit-app.com/invite_user?group_id=<group-uuid>
    ```

    Click the copy button to copy the link to your clipboard, then share it with your friend via any messaging app.

    <Warning>
      Anyone with the invite link can join the group. Share it only with people you want to give access to your music.
    </Warning>

    When your friend visits the link, they are prompted to log in (or create an account) and then automatically added to the group as a member.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Download a track">
    Any member of the group can download shared tracks. Inside the group, locate a track in the **Shared Tracks** list and click **Download**.

    The track details shown are:
    - Title and artist
    - Uploader's email
    - Upload date

    <Tip>
      Downloaded MP3 files work as **Spotify local files**. Add them to your Spotify desktop library and they sync to your playlists automatically.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
</Steps>
